# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The C4ISR Workbench serves commanders, operations staff, and intelligence analysts working from a Windows command-room environment. They need to inspect the same operational picture from different angles: command decisions, force control, communications status, computing/data health, intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance activity.

## Product Purpose

The product provides an AI-assisted C4ISR operating workspace with a tactical map, ORBAT structure, overlays, incident reports, realtime event flow, and decision-support agents. Success means users can understand force posture, inspect an area, draft reports, track assignments, and ask grounded questions without losing the audit trail or human approval path.

## Brand Personality

Serious, controlled, operational. The interface should feel like a calm command room: dense enough for repeated work, clear under pressure, and deliberate in every action.

## Anti-references

Do not make it look like a game HUD, military sci-fi dashboard, marketing page, or bright demo prototype. Avoid flashy color, decorative gradients, oversized hero content, soft glass panels, and ornamental effects that distract from map, ORBAT, and operational data.

## Design Principles

1. Map and ORBAT first: the common operating picture is the center of the product.
2. Human authority remains explicit: AI drafts, summarizes, and recommends, while critical actions require approval and audit.
3. Dense but legible: prioritize scanning, status comparison, and repeated command-room use.
4. Every layer has provenance: reports, overlays, tracks, and AI outputs must show source, time, and confidence where available.
5. Offline and degraded modes matter: the product should remain useful when communications are partial or delayed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast for operational screens. Use restrained color semantics, icon plus text for critical status, reduced-motion support, keyboard access for command palette workflows, and color-blind-safe status combinations.
